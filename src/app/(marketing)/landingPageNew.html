FAQs

Everything you need to know about FrameAI.

What is FrameAI?

FrameAI is an AI-powered video generation platform that turns text prompts into professional-quality videos. Simply describe what you want, and FrameAI handles cinematography, editing, music, and rendering — all in seconds.

Do I need video editing experience?

Not at all. FrameAI is designed for anyone — from beginners to professionals. Our AI handles the technical complexity so you can focus on your creative vision. If you can write a sentence, you can create a stunning video.

What video formats and platforms are supported?

FrameAI exports in MP4, MOV, WebM, and GIF formats. We support all major aspect ratios — 16:9 for YouTube, 9:16 for TikTok and Reels, 1:1 for Instagram, and more. All exports are optimized for their target platforms.

How long does it take to generate a video?

Most videos generate in 15–60 seconds depending on length and complexity. A 30-second marketing video typically takes under 20 seconds. Longer cinematic pieces may take up to 2 minutes. We're continuously improving generation speed.

Can I use FrameAI videos commercially?

Yes! All videos generated on paid plans include full commercial rights. You own the output and can use it for advertising, client projects, social media campaigns, or any commercial purpose. Our stock assets are fully licensed and royalty-free.

Is there a free plan available?

Yes! FrameAI's free plan includes 5 video generations per month (up to 30 seconds each), access to our core stock library, and standard export quality. No credit card required to sign up and start creating immediately.
